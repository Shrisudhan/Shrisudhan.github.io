About

Shrisudhan Govindarajan

PhD Student @ SFU, Burnaby

I am currently a Ph.D. student in GRuVi Lab, Simon Fraser University, co-advised by Prof. Andrea Tagliasacchi and Prof.Kwang Moo Yi. Before my PhD, I was a student at Indian Institute of Information Technology Madras, where I worked with Prof. Kaushik Mitra in Computational Imaging Lab. I have also had the pleasure of working with Anish Prabhu from Google XR as a part of my student research internship.

My research interest lies at the intersection of Computer vision and Computer Graphics. I am currently exploring the intersection of geometry-free and geometry-guided foundational 3D models and their applications in spatial understanding.
